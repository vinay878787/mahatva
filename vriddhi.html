Vriddhi

Transform your knowledge into Technology.

Activities based on Seven Ages Of Man, a famous monologue from William Shakespeare play “As you LIKE IT”. It describes the different stages of human life from infancy to old age using vivid imagery to convey the passage of time and various experiences one undergoes throughout their life time.
Scope: The main areas of focus for Vriddhi would be creativity, communication, imagination, logical thinking, time management, and team spirit—all of which help students explore an array of careers.
